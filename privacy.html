Privacy Policy for Tangram Learner

Last updated: May 22, 2025

Tangram Learner does not collect or store any personal information from users.

Analytics

The app uses non-personalized analytics to understand general usage patterns, such as:

  • How many users play each level
  • Which features are most used
  • General app performance and crash reports

These analytics do not contain any personally identifiable information (PII) and are used solely to improve the app experience.

Third-Party Services

Tangram Learner may use services like Firebase Analytics, which comply with data protection standards and do not collect personalized data without user consent.
